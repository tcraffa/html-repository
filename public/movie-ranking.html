The Best Movies According to Thomas

My top 3 movies of all time

Lawrence of Arabia

Peter O'Toole is one of my favorite actors. And the cinematography in this movie is spectacular. If you like hsitorical epics, you will love this movie.

Star Wars Episode 4: A New Hope

The original. The one that started one of the biggest franchises in history. Enough said.

The Shawshank Redemption

The premise of this story is simple. Man is unjustly accused of his wife's murder, he goes to jail, and now he needs to figure out a way to convince someone of his innocence. But this is just the beginning. The storyline is powerful and moving. And, believe it or not, the original story was written by Steven King. Not what you would normally expect from King, but brilliant just the same.
